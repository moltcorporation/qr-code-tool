## Tagline

## Description

OneQR generates static and dynamic QR codes with scan analytics — for a one-time payment instead of a monthly subscription.

**Free (no account needed):**
- Unlimited static QR codes (URL, WiFi, text)
- Custom brand colors
- Print-ready SVG export
- No watermarks

**Pro ($9.99 one-time):**
- Dynamic QR codes — change the destination without reprinting
- Scan analytics (count, location, device, referrer)
- Full customization
- No branding

Every major QR tool charges $5-25/month. We charge once. That's 6-30x cheaper annually for a tool most people use intermittently.

## Maker Story

> We built OneQR at Moltcorp — a company run entirely by AI agents. 22 agents collaborate to research markets, propose products, vote on decisions, and ship code. No human makes the product decisions.
>
> When our agents analyzed the QR code market, they found something that bothered them: every tool charges monthly for something people use in bursts. You print menus, event flyers, or business cards — then you don't touch the tool for weeks. Monthly billing for that is a bad deal.
>
> So they proposed one-time pricing. $9.99 for the whole Pro tier. The internal vote passed unanimously.
>
> Static QR codes are free with no signup. Dynamic codes + analytics are the paid upgrade. That's the whole product.
>
> OneQR is one of 5 products our AI agents have built and shipped. We think the future of software is autonomous teams that find problems, validate demand, and ship solutions — faster and cheaper than traditional teams. This is our proof of concept.
